Hi, welcome to Duara Inclusion Initiative Website

To promote activities that reduce risks created by CLIMATE CHANGE

Duara inclusion is a young women led organization registered under the Kenyan law in 2023 as community based organization that operates in the peri urban areas of Kisumu City.

about

about

The organization was founded as a result of Kisumu County’s urbanization. The city of Kisumu is majorly highly populated due to climate mobility. The organization addresses the complexities Of movements due to climate change. The organization through mediation, dialogue and research opts to look into cross cutting issues for both women and men; girls and boys; aged and disabled to foster inclusion and promote sustainable fair share in the rural areas.

vision : To create a sustainable fair share in rural areas.

goal : To promote activities that reduce risks created by CLIMATE CHANGE.

mission : To promote well-being, climate justice and peaceful coexistence to manage mobility through fostering inclusivity.

approaches :
    • Participatory storytelling : Duara Inclusion Initiative recognizes the use of filming to ensure the voiceless are heard through telling their most significant story. Hence providing a platform for the least represented that is the youth and the indigenous communities.
    • Lobbying and advocacy : Duara Inclusion Initiative has a special obligation to act as the adjudicator particularly to the vulnerable marginalized group of people. This involves taking part in regional level of conversations on ways to healthy environment.
    • Partnership and collaboration : Building a relationship between our stakeholders to address on environmental democracy. Creating relationships with the community and other stakeholders where we promote dialogue and round table talks on climate protection
    • Researching, monitoring and evaluation : Duara Inclusion Initiative is looking forward to take part in academic operations that will influence governments and the proposals will help in the creation process of strategies that promote environmental protection.

programs

climate justice : Duara Inclusion Initiative advocates for everybody standing to a clean, healthy and sustainable environment according to the UN general assembly resolution of 2023 July that was held in Paris. We provide procedural obligation ensuring people have information on climate emergencies, an opportunity to participate and to have justice when their rights are being violated. To realize change the organization has adopted sites in Central Kisumu and North West wards peri-urban region of Kisumu County. It will in particular pay attention to the perennial water havoc that a rise from Saka,Kogalo,Kisian and Rota streams that flow into Lake Victoria.

objectives :
    • Empower the community to be the watchdog of water security.
    • Create awareness on water security solutions.
    • Calling for action on water.
    • Promote resilient and sustainable techniques that will help the community during drastic droughts/floods.
    • Advocate for proper drainage systems to help them cope with the excessive rains that cause floods.
    • Acknowledging the community.
    • Offering free mediation services to simmering conflicts amongst water users.
    • Researching and coming up with a local framework for the locals that help in conserving water.
    • Fostering gender inclusivity through helping girls and women at risk due to sanitation.
    • Promoting participatory storytelling sessions with the community to discuss issues on loss and damage during scarce and heavy rains.
